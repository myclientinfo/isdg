Interactive Scheme Design Guide

This document is designed to be a reference for all structural engineers who are required to undertake concept or scheme designs for building structures in Australia. It is not a 'how-to' document. It is intended to be a high level document, containing material which can be easily utilised to produce accurate concept or scheme designs.

The document is arranged by material, and by building element, for quick and easy reference.

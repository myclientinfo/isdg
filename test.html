Façade engineering

Façades must fulfil a wide range of functions. They must transmit daylight to the interior, but protect against glare. The façade should facilitate cooling and heating, and prevent rain from penetrating into the building. The materials of construction must be durable under environmental extremes. Wind loads must be borne, and transmitted to the structure. Last but not least, the façade is the external image of the building that the rest of the world recognises it by.

The design of exterior wall systems depends upon many more factors than simply the type of system. The type and shape of the building structure, its inherent movements, the key façade elements, the experience of the local labour force and various other factors must be appreciated prior to any decision on the varying systems for each distinct area. 

The aim of Arup’s façade team is to provide expert advice on all aspects of building façades such that the overall objectives of appearance, performance, cost, quality, durability and programme for each project are achieved.  That is, we seek to optimise the façade solution for our clients on every project.

We suggest that façade engineering input into the design of the building façade from project inception is the ideal approach, and is the most likely means to obtaining an economical, efficient and highly serviceable façade.  By working with the project architects from an early stage, assistance on shaping of forms and selection of materials can provide direction towards a solution, which maximises the façade performance.
In response to this plethora of potentially conflicting requirements, Arup’s façade engineers have adopted the ‘total design’ approach espoused by the late Sir Ove Arup.  We believe that this is of critical importance in the design of façades, which must interact with the building structure, the building services, and project the architectural image of the building.

In addition to the design and engineering team, we have an inspection team comprised of experienced façade supervisors. They have hands-on contracting background and thus have extensive knowledge on the intricacy of façade fabrication and installations.  They are primarily deployed to observe and report on the quality of factory fabrication and site installations, and to observe whether the established QA/QC procedures have been implemented.

外墙

外墙必须满足各种功能的要求。既要考虑室内的天然采光，并减少眩光；又要考虑外墙的热工性能、防雨水渗漏性能。外墙材料必须具备在恶劣环境下使用的耐久 性；外墙系统必须能够承受风荷载，并完全将荷载传递至主体结构。外墙是一座建筑的外表，是其有别于万千广厦的视觉依据。

外墙系统的设计不只是基于系统类型的不同，而需要考虑许多因素的影响。建筑物本身类型和外形、主体结构位移、外墙主要构造元素、当地施工人员的经验以及其它因素的影响必须预先加以考虑，以确定不同地区相应外墙系统的选用。

奥雅纳外墙工程顾问的目标是提供建筑外墙的全方位专业意见，涉及范围包括建筑物总体外观、系统性能、造价、品质、耐久性和工程进度，以期为我们每一个项目的委托人提供最佳的外墙设计方案。

我们建议在项目的最初阶段，在建筑设计中同时考虑并融入外墙系统设计， 这是建成一个既符合经济原则，同时又拥有高效能和多用途的外墙系统应采用的最为理想和可行的方法。在设计初期阶段，我们就开始协助建筑师进行建筑外观设计、系统选型和选材，以充分发挥外墙系统的功能。

为了满足以上众多相互影响的性能要求，我们沿用多年来奥雅纳爵士倡导的“一体设计”方针。由于外墙设计需综合考虑建筑结构、建筑维护以及项目的建筑设计概念等因素，因此“一体设计”的方针至关重要。

除设计和工程团队以外，我们还拥有一支经验丰富的外墙监察团队。他们具有丰富的外墙产品加工、装配和安装经验，负责巡查和报告外墙制作工厂和施工现场的质量，以观察是否遵循质量标准和质量控制程序的要求。

<p>Our facade designers work in close relationship with our materials experts, enabling the development of the most innovative products for façades.</p>
